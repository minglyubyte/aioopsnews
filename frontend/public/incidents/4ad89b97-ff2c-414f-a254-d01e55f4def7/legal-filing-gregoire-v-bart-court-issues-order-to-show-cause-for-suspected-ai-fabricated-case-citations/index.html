Legal filing: Gregoire v. BART – Court issues order to show cause for suspected AI-fabricated case citations

In Gregoire v. Board of Trustees of San Francisco Bay Area Rapid Transit District, the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of California issued a second order to show cause after it could not locate three of four cases cited in Plaintiff's opposition. The court suggested these inaccuracies 'indicate the possible use of generative artificial intelligence tools resulting in hallucinations' and ordered counsel to explain the origin of each citation, whether AI was used, and verification steps.

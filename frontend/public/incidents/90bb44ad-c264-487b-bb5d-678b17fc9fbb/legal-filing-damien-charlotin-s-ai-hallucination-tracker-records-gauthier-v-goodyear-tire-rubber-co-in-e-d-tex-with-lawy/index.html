It also permitted amendment of the defective filing but warned of the severity of the misconduct.Key Judicial ReasoningThe court emphasized that attorneys remain personally responsible for the verification of all filings under Rule 11, regardless of technology used. Use of AI does not dilute the duty of candor. Continued silence and failure to rectify errors after opposing counsel flagged them exacerbated the misconduct.

Pro se litigant sanctioned $500 for citing AI-hallucinated cases in North Dakota Supreme Court brief

In City of Dickinson v. Helgeson, the Supreme Court of North Dakota found that the appellant, Seth Neil Helgeson, cited several nonexistent cases in his appellate brief. The court identified these as fictitious citations, likely generated by AI, and imposed a $500 sanction. The court affirmed the lower court's designation of Helgeson as a vexatious litigant.

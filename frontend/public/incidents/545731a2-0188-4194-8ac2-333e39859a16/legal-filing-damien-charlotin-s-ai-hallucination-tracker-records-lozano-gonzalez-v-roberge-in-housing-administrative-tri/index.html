Legal filing: Damien Charlotin's AI hallucination tracker records Lozano González v. Roberge in Housing Administrative Tribunal with Pro Se Litigant linked to alleged or found AI legal hallucination. Nature: False Quotes: Legal Norm | False translation of Articles 1960 and 1962 from the Code civil du Québec.. The landlord sought to repossess a rental property, claiming the lease renewal was suspended based on a misinterpretation of Quebec's civil code articles. He used ChatGPT to translate these articles, which resulted in a completely different meaning. The Tribunal found the repossession request invalid as it was based on a date prior to the lease's end. The Tribunal rejected the claim of abuse, accepting the landlord's sincere belief in his misinterpretation, influenced by AI translation, and noted his language barrier and residence in Mexico. The Tribunal advised the landlord to seek reliable legal advice in the future.
